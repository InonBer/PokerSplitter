Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

PokerSplitter ("we", "our", or "the app") is a home poker game tracker. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Data Stored on Your Device

All game data (players, chip counts, settlements) is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload or transmit your game history to any server.

Contacts

The app may request access to your contacts to let you quickly add player names. Contact data is used only within the app and is never transmitted or stored externally.

Purchases

PokerSplitter offers an optional Pro subscription. Purchases are processed by Apple and managed by RevenueCat. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase and usage data to manage subscriptions. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 17.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
